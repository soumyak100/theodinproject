Banana Milkshake

Photo by Charlotte May

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • Sweetener: Dates or Sugar or Honey
  • Cashews(optional, better if soaked)
  • 1tsp vanilla extract(optional)
  • 1 + 1/2 cups chilled milk
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder

Directions

  1. Take a blender and put in the peeled bananas.
  2. Add the cashews if required.
  3. Add your choice of sweetener based on your preference.
  4. Add the vanilla extract if required.
  5. Add the chilled milk.
  6. Blend everything to smooth shake.
  7. Based on preference, add milk to thin down the shake or add bananas to thicken it.
